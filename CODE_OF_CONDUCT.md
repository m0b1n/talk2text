# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful, technically serious place to collaborate.
Participants are expected to act professionally and constructively.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Focus feedback on the code, behavior, and design.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Accept that maintainers may reject changes that do not fit the project direction.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination.
- Bad-faith arguments or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors for behavior
that harms the project environment.
